Kitty Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four cats below. By clicking on a cat below you will add a specific amount of points to your score. You win the game by matching your score to the goal number. The value of each cat is not shown, you can only figure it out by adding its points to your score. Each time the game starts the value of each cat will be randomly determined.
Good luck!
